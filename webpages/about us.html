About Us

With the blessings of swami Kalyanji Maharaj started the Greenways Nursery since 19th Oct, 1992 at Faridabad n Haryana is a fully Integrated gardening organisation.

Two brother Mr. Y. P. Mourya and Dr. Mourya Singh started the Nursery with a team of professional Horticulturist, Lanscape architects, civil engineers, supervisor and trained malis. Greenways Nursery is commited to the activities of Lanscape Designing & execution and garden maintenance. We also expertises in developing garden features like rock gardens,ponds,water falls, bridges, arches, pergolas and pathways. Greenways nursery is well poised to construct greenhouses, tissue culture lab, economiclly sustainable irragation projects and herbal and medical garden. Owning a farm at Fatehpur (Delhi), Noida (U.P.) and Muzaffar nagar (U.P.) we have ample supply of indoor and outdoor plants for landscape purpose. materials to meet the requirements of plants needed in landscape projects.

Greenways deals in all type plants-trees, surbs, Climbers, Roses, Indoors, Outdoors, exiotic & common perennials bulbs annuals seedings hanging baskets and all accessories and inputs in relation to garden requirements.
